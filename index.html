Welcome To Roxy Podlogar Fine Art

Roxy Podlogar is a nationally acclaimed award-winning impressionist fine artist specializing in painting both the magnificent vistas and secluded corners of the American Southwest, particularly Northern New Mexico. She works both en plein air on location and in his studio in Santa Fe, New Mexico.
