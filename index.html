Roxy Podlogar Fine Art

I am profoundly drawn to the beauty of landscapes. I aim to capture my experience of the scenery around me through loose, expressive brush strokes. Utilizing the fluidity of watercolors, I immerse myself in the desert, seaside, or forest, painting en plein air to depict the fleeting interplay of light and shadow as it dances across the land and reflects on the water. Each piece invites the viewer to experience the moment as I felt it, where vibrant hues and dynamic strokes evoke emotion and a connection to the landscape.

Since 2016, Roxy and her husband, Carl, have sailed over 52,000 nautical miles on their catamaran, Sky Pond. They completed their circumnavigation of the Pacific Ocean in 2024. Their journey took them from Vietnam across the Pacific Ocean to Canada and Mexico before concluding in Australia. Roxy and Carl now live in Santa Fe.

During her time as a live-aboard sailor, she became a self-taught watercolorist. Despite facing the challenges of isolation, confinement, and difficult ocean passages, she utilized plein-air techniques to capture iconic landscapes. Her paintings reflect their voyages to Mexico, the Marquesas, Fiji, Vanuatu, Australia, New Zealand, and the Tuamotus.
